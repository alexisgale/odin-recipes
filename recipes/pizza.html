Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has become a global favorite. It's made with a yeasted flatbread base, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. Whether you prefer classic Margherita or loaded with toppings, pizza is always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni, bell peppers, and black olives.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Brush the edges of the dough with olive oil.
  8. If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza to the stone. If not, place the baking sheet with the pizza in the oven.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Slice and serve hot.
